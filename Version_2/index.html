I have always wanted to be a photographer for as long as I can remember. I have a husband, Corey and a son Ethan. After Ethan was born, December of 2009, I quit my job and decided to stay at home with him. My husband and I thought, what better time to start photography? Like I said, this is something I've always wanted to do, and It would keep me busy since I didn't have a job anymore (as if being a first time Mom wasn't busy enough). So, I am just now starting out. Pictures of my family mean the world to me, and I'm sure my customers feel the same about their pictures, So I try to keep my prices afordable to everyone. I specialize in family/group portraits, individual, Maternity, Newborn, Baby, Children, & Seniors. I am extremely open to any ideas that are presented to me for any photo session. My goal is to let each individuals personality shine through in their photos and Capture the Moment!
